Ellipso

2020 - present

A modern learning platform.

HTML, JavaScript, CSS, PHP, jQuery, Apache2

The Problem

Education is an individual process. Nowadays, many learning platforms are still conservative, keeping everything unified without having an eye on the unique progress of every student individually. Teachers may also be too stressed to be able to assist single students.

The Solution

Ellipso shifts the focus to the individual student, allowing them to have a more personalized experience. Also, Ellipso gives the teacher the possibility to use interactive tools, like quizzes and tests, to help students reaching the class goal.
