Ellipso Learning Platform

Education is an individual process. Nowadays, many learning platforms are still conservative, keeping everything unified without having an eye on the unique progress of every student individually. Teachers may also be too stressed to be able to assist single students.

Ellipso shifts the focus to the individual student, allowing them to have a more personalized experience. Also, Ellipso gives the teacher the possibility to use interactive tools, like quizzes and tests, to help students reaching the class goal.

Ellipso Modules

  • Video Conference Tool
    Students can join video conferences for online course meetings.
  • Substitutions
    For students: Information about substitute teachers.
    For admins: Custom and automated substitution planner.
  • Class Book
    Record students attendance, grades, etc.
  • Chat
    Easy communication between teachers, students and parents.
  • Interactive courses
    Create interactive educational games like quizzes or puzzles.
  • AI powered Learning
    Study advice and suggestions powered by AI.
  • Individual timetables
    Represents the students' individual timetables.
  • Forums
    School/class/course wide forums to share ideas and help.

Technical Features

  • Dark Mode
    Calendar supports a dark mode for users who prefer a darker theme.
  • Multiple languages
    Calendar supports multiple languages. Currently, English and German are supported
  • Responsive Design
    Calendar is designed to be used on any device.
